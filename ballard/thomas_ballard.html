Game Review

Counter Strike Global Offensive is one of the most popular FPS games in recent years. CSGO came out in the year 2012 and came as a new adapted version of the earlier game, Counter Strike Source, I have been following Counter-Strike since it's release and when it came out, it was a game like no other. In recent years it has come to a decline due to updates being very small and no gameplay changes being prominent enough to bring people back to the game or new players to the game. Until recently, it has been like this, but in December 2018 CSGO was made free for the general public and the patch before had massive changes. This has brought a new audeince to the game and I will be reviewing it up until this point. In my experience CSGO has been a very enjoyable game to play. I have many hours on the game and I can say this is a perfect game to either enjoy with your friends, be competitive with or just even having fun.
