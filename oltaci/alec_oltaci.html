FIFA Street 4 (Video Game)

FIFA Street 4 is the fourth game of the "FIFA Street" series, made by EA Sports. The series is based on the street soccer played around the world. The cartoon-like graphics have been dropped, unlike the previous versions, for a more realistic look. The graphics and player mechanisms are like FIFA 12, which was also developed by the same team that made this game. The game is made to be fast-faced, as it is usually 5 or 6-a-side. This game also has at least 50 new tricks that were not included in FIFA Street 3

    • Game Modes

    The game modes that are in fifa are knows as Kick Off, World Tour, Online Play, as well as Practice Arena. In Kick Off, there are different types of games that can be played, all specified in the kick off section of this page. In World Tour, the goal is to conquer the world with your own street soccer team. More details in the world tour section of this page. Online play is that you play against someone else online. Lastly, practice arena is that you can shoot around on a goalie, one on one, in a smaller stadium.

    Kick Off

    Kick Off allows you to play freely. You have the option to choose between futsal, panna, freestyle points, 5-a-side, or a first to X goals. You can also customize how long you wsnt the game to be, how many players per team, or even the type of ball, as well as the stadium.

    Venues

    There are many venues in the game. Some are bigger than others and the size of the net can also differ. Ranging from the streets of Amsterdam to the beaches of Rio de Janeiro, each of these arenas attempt to reflect the style of football played in that country.

    Online Play

    You can play online against other players around the world through or not through the world tour mode.

    World Tour

    The game features a World Tour mode, which lets the player create their own team, from details such as crest and team kit, to players and their individual street kits. The user then competes in competitions against the AI, after which, the total skill points each player earned in the game is tallied up in a levelling system. From levelling up players the user can use points earned to upgrade their player's skill and ability from passing to shooting to goalkeeping. With the integration of EA Sports Football Club, players can add friends' players onto their World Tour team.

    FIFA 12 Compatibility

    If you already have created players in FIFA 12 from the same console, you can link it to this game.

    Cover Player

    Just like many of the FIFA games, F.C. Barcelona and Argentine international Lionel Messi is on the cover of this game.
