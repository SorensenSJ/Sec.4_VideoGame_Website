Overwatch's Backstory

Overwatch was a group created by the United Nations to fight aginst the Omnics. There two leaders were Gabriel Reyes and Jack Morrison, there was lots tension between them. Omnics are robots with super advanced AI, at first they were good but soon after they turned evil and started creating more evil robots that were created to destroy humanity. Overwatch ended up winning the war. In its later years, Overwatch was hit with a series of allegations resulting in high-profile mission failures, corruption and mismanagement, weapons proliferation, human rights abuses and more. These issues led to worldwide protests against the once beloved Overwatch yet they still fought to protect the world. The fighting between Reyes and Morrison finally came to an explosive conclusion. In the middle of a United Nations investigation against Overwatch, a fight broke out at the Overwatch Swiss headquarters. The fighting triggered an explosion that destroyed the facility, and apparently took Reyes and Morrison into the inferno with it. After the destruction of Overwatch headquarters, the presumed deaths of Reyes and Morrison and the investigation from the United Nations, the final nail in the coffin was the Petras Act. This act declared that any actions done in the name of Overwatch would be considered a crime. Overwatch agents around the world went dark. Slowly though all of the members starting making apperances back into the world some with different identities.
