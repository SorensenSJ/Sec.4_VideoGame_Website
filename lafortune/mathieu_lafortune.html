  • Fortnite Battle Royale

    Intro

    Fortnite sets itself apart all other battle royale games with its vivid colours and outstanding multiplayer game mode.It was first released to the public in July 2017 and since then it has exploded in popularity. There has been 7 different seasons in fortnite all with different themes such as season 7 is a winter theme with new skins and an imporved map with new locations.

    Game Review

    The game starts off with 100 players in the battle bus where you can deploy yourself anywhere on the map, you then have to find guns, ammo, shields and bandage and prepare to fight to become the only person left on the map. Also in this game there is building with 3 different materials, wood, brick and metal. Building is an essential part of fortnite and is the building blocks of this game. Although building is the most important part of the game there is also alot of glitches in the game like going through walls and gets other players very upset.

    Final Rating and Conclusion


    For the final rating of this game I will be giving it a 9/10. Fortnite is a great game but no game can be perefct with all the glitching and lagging like going through walls or people dissapearing and killing you from behind.

    9/10 Rating Overall
