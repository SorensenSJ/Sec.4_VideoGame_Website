Fortnite Battle Royale

Intro

Fortnite sets itself apart all other battle royale games with its vivid colours and outstanding multiplayer game mode.It was first released to the public in July 2017 and since then it has exploded in popularity. There has been 7 different seasons in fortnite all with different themes such as season 7 is a winter theme with new skins and an imporved map with new locations.

Game Review

The game starts off with 100 players in the battle bus where you can deploy yourself anywhere on the map, you then have to find guns, ammo, shields and bandage and prepare to fight to become the only person left on the map.
