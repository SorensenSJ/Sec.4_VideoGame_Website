My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic-Review

By:Gabriel Vincenti

What is it about?

My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic is a video game that was based on its television series. It revolves around franchise and the life of magical ponies that work together to create a happy and peaceful community and fight crime. The goal of the game is to build your own city; making houses, farms, parks, etc, and making it a success.

  1. Characters: Rainbow Dash, Rarity, Fluttershy, Pinkie Pie, and Applejack.
  2. Setting: Fairytale Land.

Who is it For?

This video game is intended for children. More specifically young girls. Although I believe teens would also enjoy this video game as it deals with a franchise, businesses, etc.

Platforms Available On

  1. iPhone
  2. iPad
  3. xBox One/360
  4. Playstation 4/3

Review/Rating: Is It Worth Buying?

In my Opinion, My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, is a videogame definetly worth buying. The rating this game deserves is a 8/10. It is very fun to play. It allows you to use and enhance your Imagination. Although, there are always some cons to a videogame...
